HOW TO ADD  —  ZODIAC SERIES
============================

1) Save each Zodiac painting into THIS folder ( images/zodiac/ ).
   Use a simple lowercase .jpg name, e.g.  zodiac-aries.jpg , zodiac-leo.jpg …
   Tip: crop to just the artwork (frame is fine). ~1200px on the long edge.

2) Open index.html, find the "Zodiac series" spot in the works list
   (near the bottom), and add ONE line per painting. Point img at this
   folder with the  zodiac/  prefix, and keep the zodiac tag:

   {img:"zodiac/zodiac-aries.jpg", title:"Aries", desc:"One line.", medium:"Acrylic on canvas · 60 × 80 cm · 2025", tags:["zodiac"]},

That's it — it appears under the "Zodiac" tab (and under "All") automatically.
Until you add a line, the Zodiac tab shows a tasteful "arriving soon" message,
so nothing ever looks broken.
